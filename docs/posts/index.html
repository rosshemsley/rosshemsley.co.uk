Why I started avoiding the word 'own'

I have started trying to avoid the word “own” in the workplace. Admittedly, it’s a bit of a silly idea, but I do think it’s a worthwhile adjustment that can help improve our professional relationships. This little word can pop up quite a lot, “Jane owns this project”, “I own this document”, “Pete can you own this?”. It feels innocent enough, but I would argue that it can nudge our mindset in the wrong direction in the workplace.
